Usage

Use the /suggestion route with any of the following querystring params:

n: The number of desired results

q: The city's name

population: The city's population.

latitude: The city's latitude.

longitude: The city's longitude.

closeto: Search for city's near this one

eg: /suggestions?q=red&closeto=Edmonton&population=20000
